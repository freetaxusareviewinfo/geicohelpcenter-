GEICO Insurance is a popular American company that mainly provides auto insurance.  
It is known for affordable rates, easy online quotes, and 24/7 customer support.  
GEICO also offers home, renters, and motorcycle insurance coverage.


Step-by-Step Guide
==================

1. Go to the official GEICO website:  
   `https://www.geico.com <https://www.geico.com>`_

2. Click on **“Get a Quote”** at the top of the page.

3. Choose the type of insurance you need (auto, home, renters, etc.).

4. Enter your ZIP code to begin the quote process.

5. Fill in your personal details such as name, date of birth, and address.

6. Provide information about your vehicle or property.

7. Answer questions about your driving history or coverage needs.

8. Review the coverage options suggested by GEICO.

9. Compare prices and select the plan that fits your budget.

10. Save or print your quote for future reference.
